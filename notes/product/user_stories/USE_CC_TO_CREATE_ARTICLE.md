# Creating and Publish an Article with Audio

Visit https://capitalcopilot.io and click on 'News' in the side navigation.

Click on Fetch URLs to get a fresh list of News URLs.

Select the Team you want to post the article to.

Select the News URLs you want to include in the Article.

Click on Create Article Audio.

Once the Article is generated a message will provide a link to that new Article.
- Click the link to go to the new Article.
NOTE: You can do this even while the audio is being generated as it will also be available from the ARticle page once the audio is generated.

Wait until the audio is generated and then click on the Play button to listen to the audio.

Make any changes to the Article as needed.
- Update the title or content in the Article.
- Click the Update button.
- Click Generate Audio to create a new audio file.
- Listen to the new audio file, make changes, and repeat as necessary.

Once the Article is ready, click on the Publish to Telegram or check the Live on Podcast checkbox to make the audio go live.
